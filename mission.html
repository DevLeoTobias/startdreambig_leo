ABOUT US

OUR VISION

Inspiring kids to Dream Big by exposing them to an environment that unleashes their own potential. Disciplining the next generation of community leaders to grow spiritually and gain life skills, who will lead in their homes, workplaces and communities.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create opportunities for kids (4th – 8th grade), in the greater Sacramento by exposing them to Coding and hands-on Robotics regardless of socio-economic status. Allowing them to discover a world of amazing possibilities through learning basic computer code and engineering principles all while having fun.

WE BELIVE

  • Kids are capable of creating the incredible

  • Hands on tasks accelerate the learning process and give life skills

  • Together we can do Great Things

  • Exposing ones own abilities, increases self-esteem, which leads to a brighter future!

LET'S INSPIRE TOGETHER

Building critical thinkers and problem solvers of tomorrow. Partnering with churches, schools, community groups and non-profit organizations to bring hope and equal opportunity for all.

First of its kind faith-based 501 (c) (3) nonprofit organization located in Sacramento California dedicated to giving every kid/student in underrepresented communities the opportunity to learn computer programming and robotics.

Statement of Faith

Start Dream Big affirms the Bible as the inspired and authoritative word of God and, with the Holy Spirit’s inspiration, the absolute reference point for every aspect of life and ministry. Based upon God’s word, who He is, and His initiative of salvation through the atoning work of Jesus (His death, burial, and resurrection).
