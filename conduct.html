Code of Conduct

Start Dream Big is conducting programs which will provide positive, constructive, recreational and educational activities for your son, daughter or ward. Each activity is designed to reach valuable life skills to its member. Start Dream Big staff expects your child to act in a responsible and respectful way towards other and instructors, coaches and volunteers. Therefore it is imperative that your child adheres to the safety policies we have set forth. To the participant:

I will maintain a positive attitude at all times.
I will dress appropriately during all activities.
I will respect myself and others at all times.
I will not say racist or prejudicial remarks.
I will not use profanity, will not act physically or verbally abusive or become violent with others.
I will respect all equipment, supplies, and materials.
I will not leave the schedule activity until I notify all staff members present and after receiving permission from staff member in charge.
I will be on time for all schedule meetings, activities.
I will report any problem to the staff.
I will follow all safety instructions at all time.

Any violation of the aforementioned rules will result in corrective action being taken. We are looking forward to a positive experience. It is the responsibility of Start Dream Big staff to maintain a safe environment for our students. Therefore we cannot allow the behavior of any individual to jeopardize the success and safety of our program. The corrective action is as follow: Oral reprimand, Written reprimand, Dismissal from our program.
