FOUNDERS

Husband and Wife Team

Jeferson and Suzanne met in Brazil, in 2005, where together they worked in a non-profit organization (YWAM) as missionaries helping street kids re-enter society through restoration houses and community centers. After getting married, they moved to California where they are raising their two daughters. They have always had a heart for the next generation, to encourage, teach and inspire them to be all they can be and more!

About Jeferson

Jeferson Tobias is a tech entrepreneur who created his own start-up. Born in Brazil, Sao Paulo, Jeferson grew up during a time when computers were only accessable to the rich. At the age of 15, when he obtained his first job, Jeferson bought his first computer. He began to teach himself computer code such as Delphi Pascal, Clipper and others. In 1999 he Graduated in Computer Programming and continued on to pursue Computer Science until 2002. Today Jeferson proudly works as a Software Engineer at Bradford Software Technologies in San Jose, California.

About Suzanne

Suzanne Tobias is a sign language interpreter for the State of California. Raised in Northern California, she has always been a creative, recieving her first camera at 7, photography quickly became a great passion that has led to documenting everything from weddings, births, concert events and more. Her artistic talent has also given her an eye for graphic design, creating marketing materials for events, magazine ads, social media and websites.
