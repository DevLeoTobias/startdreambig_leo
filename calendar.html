Our Classes

We provide FREE classes for those who are looking to learn code through robotics, giving young people opportunities to recognize and develop their own unique creativity by building and programming a robot.

Workshops are a great opportunity for students to learn basic programming, get introduced to Arduino and discover the wonders of robotics. Using a programming language based off of Scratch, these workshop are easy for students to program and learn hands-on for themselves how real life robots and coding works.
Our Workshops instill a foundation to Create, Explore and increase Self-Esteem

Grades: 4th - 8th

Duration: 2 hours

Max of Participants per workshop: 14

* Robot Kits & Laptops are provided for use during the workshop/class.
